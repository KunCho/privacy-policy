隐私政策

欢迎使用 EasyReading（以下简称“本应用”）。我们非常重视您的隐私与数据安全。本隐私政策旨在向您说明应用在使用过程中如何收集、使用、存储与保护您的信息。

一、我们如何收集与使用信息

1. 本应用不会收集您的个人身份信息（如姓名、手机号、邮箱等）。

2. 您导入到应用中的本地电子书文件仅保存在您的设备本地，用于阅读与进度记录，不会被上传到任何服务器。

3. 应用可能读取存储权限以便扫描并导入本地 EPUB 文件，该权限仅用于上述目的。

二、数据存储与安全

1. 书籍元数据、阅读进度、阅读设置等数据会保存在本地数据库或本地偏好设置中。

2. 我们采取合理的技术手段尽可能保障本地数据安全，但请您同时妥善保管设备与应用。

三、第三方服务

本应用目前不接入任何第三方统计、广告或分析 SDK，也不会向第三方分享您的数据。

四、您的权利

1. 您可以随时在应用中删除导入的书籍及相应数据。

2. 您可以卸载应用以删除所有本地保存的数据。

五、隐私政策的更新

我们可能会根据产品功能与法律法规的变化对本政策进行更新。更新后的内容将以应用内展示为准。

六、联系我们

如对本隐私政策有任何疑问或建议，请通过应用市场反馈渠道与我们联系。

Privacy Policy

Welcome to EasyReading (hereinafter referred to as "the App"). We value your privacy and data security. This Privacy Policy explains how the App collects, uses, stores, and protects your information during use.

1. Information Collection and Use

1. The App does not collect your personal identity information (such as name, phone number, email, etc.).

2. The eBook files you import into the App are stored only on your device, used for reading and progress tracking, and will not be uploaded to any server.

3. The App may request storage permission in order to scan and import local EPUB files. This permission is used solely for this purpose.

2. Data Storage and Security

1. Book metadata, reading progress, and reading settings are stored in the local database or local preferences.

2. We take reasonable technical measures to protect local data security. However, please also safeguard your device and the App properly.

3. Third-Party Services

The App does not integrate any third-party analytics, advertising, or SDKs, nor does it share your data with third parties.

4. Your Rights

1. You may delete imported books and related data within the App at any time.

2. You may uninstall the App to remove all locally stored data.

5. Updates to this Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time according to changes in product features and legal requirements. The updated version will be displayed within the App.

6. Contact Us

If you have any questions or suggestions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us through the app marketplace feedback channel.
